Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2025

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, here's how we can help.

Return Eligibility

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
  • Items purchased during clearance or flash sales are non-returnable unless defective.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used hair care products (for hygiene reasons).
  • Custom-made wigs or hair patches.
  • Digital downloads.

How to Initiate a Return

Contact us at support@luvkushnatural.com or via WhatsApp with your order number and reason for return. We'll arrange a pickup at no extra cost for eligible returns.

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are via bank transfer.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We'll replace or refund at no charge.
