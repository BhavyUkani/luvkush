Shipping Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2025

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹499 across India.

Shipping Charges

  • Orders below ₹499: ₹49 flat shipping fee.
  • Express delivery (2–3 business days): ₹99 regardless of order value.

Delivery Timelines

  • Metro cities: 3–5 business days (standard)
  • Tier 2 / Tier 3 cities: 5–7 business days (standard)
  • Remote areas: 7–10 business days

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday–Saturday, 10 AM – 5 PM IST. Orders placed after 5 PM or on Sundays are processed the next business day.

Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with the tracking link. You can also track your order from the My Orders section.

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned due to an incorrect address or repeated failed delivery attempts, we'll contact you to reschedule. Reshipping charges may apply.
