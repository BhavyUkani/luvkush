Our Story

From Our Grandmothers'
Kitchen to Your Home

Luv Kush Natural was born from a simple truth: the most effective hair care formulas were already in our grandmothers' kitchens. Cold-pressed amla, slow-infused bhringraj, fresh-ground brahmi — these weren't exotic treatments. They were rituals.

We founded Luv Kush Natural to honour these rituals and make them accessible to every Indian household. Our oils and treatments are formulated exactly as they would have been centuries ago — without shortcuts, synthetic preservatives, or marketing gimmicks.
